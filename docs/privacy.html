Privacy Policy

The Dutch Flyfisher — Instagram Content Publishing App

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Overview

This application is used exclusively by the owner of the Instagram account @thedutchflyfisher to publish photos to their own Instagram account via the Meta Content Publishing API.

Data collection

This application does not collect, store, or share any personal data from third parties. No user accounts, no tracking, no analytics.

The only data used is the owner's own Instagram access token, which is stored locally on the owner's machine and is never transmitted to any third-party service.

Third-party services

The application communicates exclusively with the Meta Graph API (developers.facebook.com) on behalf of the account owner.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to the account owner via Instagram: @thedutchflyfisher.
